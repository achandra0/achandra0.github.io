Chocolate Truffle Cake

This cake is a wonderful treat for chocolate lovers!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 3 tablespoon coco
  • 1.5 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup refined oil
  • 1 cup sugar

Method

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients in Bowl 1 and wet ingredients in Bowl 2.
  2. Set your handmixer on high and mix the ingredients of Bowl 1 with Bowl 2.
  3. Once the mixture is evenly mixed, set your baking tray and pre heat the oven.
  4. Bake the cake in oven at 300 degrees for 45 minutes
  5. Once baked, wait for the cake to cool down. Top it up with some whipped cream and attack the cake!

Caesar Salad

An all time classic, but, with a shortcut!

Ingredients

  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bunch of romaine lettuce
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon grated parmesan for garnishing
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 cups cubed bread
  • 1/4 cup olive oil

Method

  1. Mince half of garlic. In a bowl, combine mayo, mustard, minced garlic and lemon juice.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet. Throw in remaining garlic and remove once they are brown.
  3. Add bread cubes to the skillet, tossing frequently. Remove from the skillet once they are golden brown.
  4. Season the break cubes with salt and pepper
  5. Break the lettuce in bite sized pieces. Toss it with the dressing, parmesan cheese and bread cubes. Enjoy!
